Marquee Hire Prices

One of the most important factors when hiring a marquee is choosing the correct size. The right size helps create the perfect atmosphere, avoiding a space that feels too empty or one that becomes crowded and uncomfortable.

We would advise on between 12sq ft and 15sq ft per person for weddings etc when seated at round tables, and 6sq ft when guests are standing for a buffet style function.

⚠️ Prices are for marquee only and exclude Delivery & VAT

For additional products such as flooring, linings, lights, heaters and furniture, please call or email for a full detailed quote.
